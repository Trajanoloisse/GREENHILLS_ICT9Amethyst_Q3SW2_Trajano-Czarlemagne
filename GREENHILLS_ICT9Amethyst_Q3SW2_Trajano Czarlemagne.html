Sabrina Spellman discovers on her sixteenth birthday that she is a witch. With the help of her witch aunts, Hilda and Zelda, and their talking cat, Salem, Sabrina learns to master her powers. Directed by Lee Toland Krieger

Character Description Played by
Sabrina Spellman - A teenage half-witch, half-mortal who is determined to do whatever it takes to be a normal human. Kiernan Shipka
Zelda Spellman - Zelda is a full witch. She is the older sister of Hilda Spellman and the aunt of Sabrina. Elizabeth Alice Broderick
Hilda Spellman - Hilda Spellman is, like her older sister, a full witch. She is also one of the aunts of Sabrina. Lucy Davis
